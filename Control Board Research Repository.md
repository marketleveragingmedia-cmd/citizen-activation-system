### Article #20
**Title:** The Retirement Crisis: Is the 401(k) To Blame? - CBS News  
**URL:** https://www.cbsnews.com/news/the-retirement-crisis-is-the-401k-to-blame/  
**Description:** CBS analysis arguing 401(k) plans are not to blame for retirement crisis - the real culprits are inadequate savings and risky investment strategies. Required savings: 12-15% of pay annually for 40 years (ages 25-65). US savings rate only ~5%, hasn't reached 12% since early 1970s. Most people take excessive equity risk (trying to compensate for low savings), then lose 50% in financial crises. Same issues affect public pension plans with underfunding and short contribution cycles. Bottom line: save more, take less risk.
